The murder was committed in Kercher and Knox’s rental apartment which they shared with two Italian students while attending classes at the University for Foreigners at the University of Perugia. On the night of November 1, 2007,Kercher was found on the floor of her bedroom, strangled, stabbed in the neck and partially unclothed with 47 cuts and bruises on her body (Kington 1). Blood was found at the scene and in the bathroom. According to the London Guardian, the window was broken from the inside, suggesting an attempt to fake a robbery. Footprints were also found under the body. The murder weapon, a bloody knife, was left at the scene.
